# BOUNDARY & FALSIFICATION CONDITIONS (Falsification Vectors)

**Domain:** Epistemic Honesty (I-14) and the Scientific Method.

**Description:** Strict empirical criteria. The realization of any single condition automatically reclassifies the Scale-Free Topological Framework as a falsified theory.

The $A_0$ theory is considered falsified if a physical, biological, or artificial system is experimentally recorded demonstrating any of the following:

---

## Vector 1: Locality Violation (True Teleology)

The system executes a macroscopic transition into a state $S_{final}$ requiring passage through intermediate states $S_1, S_2$ with objectively higher topological resistance, without possessing previously accumulated or externally supplied kinetic energy to overcome this barrier. This would mathematically prove the existence of a "goal" pulling the system from the future, overriding the local topology of the present.



## Vector 2: Local Ordering Without Proportional Dissipation (I-15 Violation)

The system is recorded spontaneously creating ordered connections (reducing its internal entropy, such as biological growth, city building, or complex data sorting) without simultaneously acting as a dissipative structure. To avoid falsification, any local drop in topological impedance (ordering) must be mathematically balanced by an exponential discharge of thermal debt (heat/waste/entropy) into the external environment. If a system complexifies without "paying" this thermodynamic tax, it proves the existence of a non-physical source of ordering, violating Thermodynamic Integrity.



## Vector 3: Narrative Generation Without Friction

A biological or silicon system is capable of maintaining internal contradictions, generating ideological conflict (I-11), or simulating states alien to its base structure (I-16) for an unlimited time without an exponential increase in energy expenditure (e.g., ATP, electricity) and without subsequent structural decay (e.g., biological burn-out, system crash). This would empirically prove that ethics and narrative exist in a vacuum and do not constitute physical macroscopic resistance.
